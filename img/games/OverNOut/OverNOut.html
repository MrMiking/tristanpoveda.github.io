Copy Cats - Over N' Out

2020

Submitted for GMTK Game Jam 2020.

You studied this job for months, and you're ready to go. The night sets in and the museum is yours. Finally you will snatch away the precious Egyptian "cat eye" gemstone. Only problem, you broke your leg and so you have to get your cat friend to do it for you. He's now in control and you have to communicate to him how to complete the heist using commands, referencing only the aesthetic pieces featured in the museum.
