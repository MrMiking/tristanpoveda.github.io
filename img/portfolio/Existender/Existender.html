Existender

Final 3D Video Project for 3D course in Faculty of Fine Arts at University of Porto.

January 2017

The project consisted of inventing and modeling a fictional paradoxical or impossible object in Blender 3D, and promoting it with a set of advertisement Posters and a Video.
