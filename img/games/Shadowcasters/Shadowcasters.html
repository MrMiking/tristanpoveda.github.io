Shadowcasters

November 2019

Game developed for the Teesside Games Creation Society Jam in 2019, developed in Unity in 32 hours. First Place Winner!

In the spirit of Halloween, witches use their spells to cast out the shadows and battle it out in this bomberman inspired game!

Inspired by Jakub Wasilewski shadow shader implementation in PICO-8.

Roles included: art and game design.
