Interstellar Inspired Short Film

3D Video Project for 3D course in Faculty of Fine Arts at University of Porto.

January 2017

The goal was to create a full video to accompany a song of our choosing. I chose Hans Zimmer soundtrack from Interstellar and decided to make an interpretation of the launch sequence from the movie.

The entire video was created with blender, and featured a simple smoke simulation, an earth atmosphere material, a nebula simulation, recreating Saturn and finally a black hole created just with shaders.
