Procedural Stylized Trees

A Houdini system to generate stylized trees.

To be used in any game engine as a tool to ease level design and environment art.

2019

The Houdini asset generates variations of trees with a painterly stylized look. All the normals face outwards to achieve the stylized shading effect when lit.
